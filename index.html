Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How accurate is the ATS analysis?

Our AI analyzes your resume using the same criteria as modern ATS systems, providing 85-90% accuracy in compatibility scoring.

Is my resume data secure?

Yes! All resume data is processed locally in your browser and never stored on our servers. Your privacy is our priority.

What file formats are supported?

We support PDF and DOCX files up to 10MB. These are the most common resume formats used by job seekers.

How long does analysis take?

Analysis typically completes within 10-15 seconds, providing you with instant feedback and actionable recommendations.
